Get an extended look at Guardians of the Galaxy!

By Amanda Wood on July 10, 2014

We just got a special extended look at next month’s Guardians of the Galaxy, and it’s safe to say this is going to be a unique take on the superhero genre.

First of all, the clip is set to another track off of Starlord’s “Awesome Mix Vol. 1,” “Cherry Bomb” by The Runaways, which means we love it by default. The soundtrack features lots of other classics like “Hooked on a Feeling” by Blue Swede (heard in earlier trailers) and “I Want You Back” by the Jackson 5, so it’s probably worth seeing for the soundtrack alone.

We also got a pretty good look at each individual character in the Guardians group, with Rocket the raccoon (brilliantly voiced by Bradley Cooper) given more time to shine. He may look like an ordinary raccoon, but he’s definitely the scrappiest rodent in the galaxy from what we can see. Rocket has always been a fan favourite, so it’s exciting to see him in all his fierce glory.

Vin Diesel provides the voice of tree-like alien Groot, who we also got a more in-depth look at. If you don’t know his name now, don’t worry: all he can say is “I am Groot”, so you’ll probably know it by the end of the movie. His alien powers consist of making spiky roots shoot up out of his back (as a defense mechanism, we guess) and growing various flowers and vines on his body at will. He also eats some kind of plant off his arm, so there’s that.

The rest of the gang’s all here too in this clip, with the rest of the notorious Guardians being rounded out by Starlord (Chris Pratt), Gamora (Zoe Saldana) and Drax (Dave Bautista). Lee Pace’s villain Ronan the Accuser is joined by Karen Gillan as Nebula and Josh Brolin as Thanos to threaten the galaxy so that the Guardians can guard it.

Guardians of the Galaxy hits Cineplex theatres August 1st. Check out the galactic adventures below in this new clip!
